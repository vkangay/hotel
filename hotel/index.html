About Us

Nectar Hotel has a classic and elegant language, punctuated by contemporary furniture, works of art, objects and antiques. It is these pieces, the light, the comfort of the fabrics and materials used, and the treatment of the space as if it were a residence, which intend to distinguish this from all other hotels that may exist in the surroundings, creating a unique place, like a “Oasis of comfort and discreet luxury”. The Nectar Hotel welcomes leisure and business guests, offering a space in a kind of enlarged house, with a deeply intimate atmosphere.
